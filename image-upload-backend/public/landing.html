How it Works:

Meet your new favorite online loyalty program! Stampd incentivises users to visit local businesses by provifing digital stamp cards to a user. A stamp is added to the card for every valid purchase. When the specified amount of stamps are reached, a reward is given, improving customer retention for the business and allowing customers to be rewarded for supporting local businesses!
